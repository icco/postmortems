A planned outage for the .COM/.NET SRS Production Environment occurred on February 9th, from 01:00 am to 01:45 am UTC. The duration of this scheduled event was 45 minutes.

During this period, several services were potentially unavailable to customers. These included availability checks, Whois services, domain purchases, domain renewals, and domain updates.

The root cause of the service disruption was scheduled system maintenance performed by the registry.

The maintenance was completed within the specified 45-minute window, resolving the potential unavailability of the affected services.
